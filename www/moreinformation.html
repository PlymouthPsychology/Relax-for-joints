### About this app The RRR app (Relaxation for Rapid Recovery) is a prototype, intended for use in the NHS for patients undergoing joint replacement. It was developed jointly by Plymouth University, with colleagues from the NHS. Although we are currenty evaluating the app, patients are welcome to use it, and we would be grateful for feedback. If you found the app useful, or have suggestions for improvement please please contact: [ben.whalley@plymouth.ac.uk](mailto:ben.whalley@plymouth.ac.uk)
